About Us

_________

The Ice Cream Company is an old-fashioned neighborhood ice cream shop. Whether you want a scoop of your favorite flavor, an awesome lunch or a rich, creamy milkshake you’ve found the right place! We always aim to please with great food, a friendly atmosphere and personal service.


At the Ice Cream Company, we treat everyone who walks through the door of our shop like a regular customer, whether it’s your first time or you’ve been coming here since the beginning. Many people have found memories of enjoying a delicious cone beneath our famous hand carved wooden train!
